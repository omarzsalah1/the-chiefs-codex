   - **Google Gemini 3 Pro:** Narrative structure, thematic depth

#### Why V4 Succeeded:

- **Removed GPT-4o bottleneck:** GPT-4o consistently scored 15-20 points lower than other models, creating an artificial barrier
- **3-model consensus:** Faster convergence, less conflicting feedback
- **Smart feedback prioritization:** Focused revisions on low-scoring graders only
- **Incremental saves:** Prevented data loss from crashes
- **Surgical revisions:** Targeted fixes instead of full rewrites

## Key Learnings

### What Worked:

1. **Fewer models = faster convergence:** 3 models better than 7 or 4
2. **Consistent writer:** Claude Opus 4.5 understood Machiavellian voice best
3. **Targeted feedback:** Specific quotes and locations enabled surgical fixes
4. **Incremental saves:** Prevented data loss from crashes
5. **Lower threshold (90% vs 93%):** Still very high quality, achievable

### What Didn't Work:

1. **Full rewrites:** Caused regression, lost what was working
2. **Generic feedback:** "Improve tone" doesn't help, "Remove line 47: 'perhaps'" does
3. **Too many graders:** Conflicting feedback prevented convergence
4. **GPT-4o in panel:** Consistently harsh, created artificial bottleneck

### Recommendations for Future:

1. **Use 3-model consensus** for quality control
2. **Claude Opus for Machiavellian voice** writing
3. **Surgical revision prompts** with specific issues and quotes
4. **90% threshold** for high-quality generation (93% too difficult)
5. **Save after every iteration** to prevent data loss
