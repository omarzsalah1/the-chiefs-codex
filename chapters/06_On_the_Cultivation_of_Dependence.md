### I. On the Nature of Nearness

The servant errs in believing that to touch the hem is to guide the hand. This vanity consumes the capable while the hollow survive.

The prince is the fire. He who stands within the flame cannot perceive its shape; only from the shadows is the burning structure visible. Proximity breeds blindness. The closer one stands to the light, the deeper the dark.

The gatekeeper mistakes the door for the house. When the master tires of the portal, he hews a new passage through the stone, leaving the keeper guarding nothing but air.
